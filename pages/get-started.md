# Getting Started

UniqID makes it easy to bring hardware-based bot detection into your application stack.

## What you will find here

- An overview of the integration workflow
- Recommended deployment patterns
- How to configure your initial protection policies

## Next steps

1. Review your traffic flows and decide where bot verification should occur.
2. Select the UniqID protection level that matches your risk profile.
3. Integrate the UniqID API with your login, signup, or transaction endpoints.
4. Monitor the first deployment and tune policies for false-positive balance.
